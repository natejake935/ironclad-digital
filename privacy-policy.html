Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 13, 2026

Ironclad Digital ("Ironclad Digital," "we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information submitted through our website, forms, booking tools, and communication systems.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Name
  • Business name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Website URL
  • Service details or project information
  • Appointment or booking information
  • Messages submitted through website forms

We may also collect basic technical information such as browser type, device information, IP address, and website usage data for security, analytics, and performance improvement.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Respond to inquiries
  • Provide website, automation, booking, and communication services
  • Send appointment confirmations, scheduling updates, and service-related notifications
  • Route lead information to the appropriate business or account owner
  • Improve our website, software, and customer experience
  • Maintain security and prevent misuse

3. SMS Communications

By submitting a form, booking an appointment, or otherwise providing your phone number, you may consent to receive SMS communications from Ironclad Digital or from businesses using Ironclad Digital services.

SMS messages may include:

  • Appointment confirmations
  • Scheduling reminders
  • Technician or service updates
  • Customer support messages
  • Lead or form submission notifications
  • Post-service follow-up or review requests

Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP. For help, reply HELP or contact us through our website.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

We may share information only when necessary to:

  • Provide our services
  • Route customer inquiries to the appropriate business
  • Support website hosting, analytics, messaging, or software infrastructure
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights, users, or systems

Mobile opt-in data and SMS consent information will not be sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and maintain business records.

7. Your Choices

You may request to update, access, or delete your information by contacting us. You may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP.

8. Third-Party Services

Our website and services may use third-party providers for hosting, analytics, communications, payment processing, scheduling, or automation. These providers process information only as needed to support our services.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Ironclad Digital website.
